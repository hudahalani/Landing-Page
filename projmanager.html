Project Manager - Accessibility Engineering Project

University of Toronto Engineering Curriculum

Project Overview

As part of the engineering curriculum at the University of Toronto, I served as project manager for a team of five engineers tasked with creating innovative solutions to real-world accessibility challenges within a UofT building. This project required comprehensive problem analysis, creative solution development, and effective project management to deliver a practical, inclusive design solution.

Problem Statement

The project focused on addressing accessibility challenges within a University of Toronto building, requiring our team to:

  • Identify Barriers: Analyze existing accessibility issues within the building
  • User-Centered Design: Consider the needs of individuals with various disabilities
  • Innovative Solutions: Develop creative approaches to accessibility challenges
  • Practical Implementation: Ensure solutions are feasible and cost-effective
  • Compliance Standards: Meet accessibility guidelines and regulations

Project Management Role

Leadership Responsibilities

As project manager, I was responsible for overseeing all aspects of the project:

  • Project Planning: Developed comprehensive project timelines and milestones
  • Team Coordination: Facilitated effective communication and collaboration among team members
  • Resource Management: Allocated tasks and resources efficiently across the team
  • Progress Monitoring: Tracked project milestones and ensured timely delivery
  • Quality Assurance: Maintained high standards for all deliverables

Communication Management

Established and maintained effective communication channels:

  • Team Meetings: Regular coordination meetings to discuss progress and challenges
  • Stakeholder Communication: Regular updates to faculty advisors and clients
  • Documentation: Comprehensive project documentation and progress reports
  • Presentation Skills: Led team presentations to faculty and stakeholders

Technical Implementation

Design Process

Utilized professional engineering tools and methodologies:

  • AutoCAD Proficiency: Created detailed technical drawings and floor plans
  • 3D Modeling: Developed comprehensive 3D models of proposed solutions
  • Technical Documentation: Produced detailed specifications and implementation guides
  • Cost Analysis: Conducted thorough cost-benefit analysis of proposed solutions

Solution Development

The team developed innovative accessibility solutions including:

  • Mobility Solutions: Improved access for individuals with mobility challenges
  • Visual Accessibility: Enhanced navigation for visually impaired users
  • Hearing Accessibility: Improved communication systems for hearing-impaired individuals
  • Universal Design: Solutions that benefit all users, regardless of ability

Project Deliverables
