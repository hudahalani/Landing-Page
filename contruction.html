Construction Course

During high school, I took a construction course where I explored hands-on projects and design. I used Google SketchUp to create detailed models of various wood structures, such as a cutting board and drywall, which allowed me to become proficient with power tools and understand essential building etiquette and safety protocols. I also designed a functioning electrical circuit that powered a light bulb, showcasing my ability to apply theoretical knowledge to practical applications, attached here (I actually built/wired them but I don't have pictures of those oops).
